BABYGIRL (2023)

A provocative psychological drama starring Nicole Kidman. Written and directed by Halina Reijn.

Synopsis

Babygirl is a daring exploration of power dynamics and forbidden desire. Nicole Kidman delivers a career-defining performance as a successful CEO who enters into a dangerous relationship with her much younger intern.

Written and directed by Halina Reijn, the film examines the complexities of modern relationships with unflinching honesty and visual poetry. Premiered at the Cannes Film Festival to critical acclaim.
